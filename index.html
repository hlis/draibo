DRAIBO - What we do

DRAIBO is a project aiming to help folks wishing to customize their electronics. Customization can range from additional functionality, modification of the default parts to simply styling your device

PORTABLES

Portables

Your portable TV can be modded in several ways, removing limits inherent in the default configuration, changing the antenas for better reception, larger screens with better picture quality, as well as audio improvements. CAMERAS

Cameras

...as well. Cameras can be modded to improve picture quality by changing parts like the lens, or improving battery life by both swapping batteries to modern rechargeable batteries, as well as setting the camera to work in a more battery mindful mode. Even...

CONSOLES

Consoles

...have a (big) place. Consoles can be modified either to simply style it however you like it, to adding further functions to allow you to rock in your favorite games. Portable devices can also have their batteries changed to better ones as well.

DRAIBO - About us

DRAIBO was formed in 2010 by a group of retro tech aficionados when we were all in college. We started tinkering with old tech to bring it back up even if a bit to modern standards, and we enjoy doing it.
